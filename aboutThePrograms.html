About EDU ICT

About Bachelor of Education (ICT)

    About the Program

    The Bachelor of Education in ICT at Mzuzu University prepares students to become professional educators with strong ICT skills. The program combines information and communication technology knowledge with teaching methods, enabling graduates to effectively integrate technology into classrooms and learning environments.

Program Goals:

  • Equip future teachers with practical ICT skills for education.
  • Develop the ability to design, implement, and manage ICT-based learning resources.
  • Enhance digital literacy for teaching, learning, and administration in schools.
  • Foster critical thinking and problem-solving skills for ICT challenges in education.

Career Opportunities:

    Graduates can work as:

  • ICT teachers in secondary or tertiary institutions
  • Educational technology specialists
  • Curriculum developers integrating ICT
  • Trainers in government, NGOs, or private institutions

Program Features:

  • Hands-on ICT labs and educational technology projects
  • Training in pedagogy and classroom technology integration
  • Exposure to modern teaching tools, software, and online learning platforms
  • Guidance from experienced faculty in education and ICT
